Recommended Solution Overviews

RSOs are earned, not sold.

Recommended Solution Overviews are downstream execution artifacts. They are produced only after operational reality is verified.

What an RSO Is

  • • An operator-grade narrative grounded in evidence
  • • A bridge between verified reality and execution
  • • Suitable for exec, board, and outbound use

What an RSO Is Not

  • • Not discovery
  • • Not strategy formulation
  • • Not a pitch deck
  • • Not a substitute for understanding

Why you can’t buy an RSO yet

RSOs do not create clarity. They express it.

Without Module One, any RSO would be built on assumptions — and assumptions scale failure faster than execution ever could.

Module One establishes what is true, what is constrained, and what is unknowable. Only then is an RSO valid.
