RECOMMENDED SOLUTION OVERVIEW

The RSO

The RSO is a downstream execution artifact. It does not discover reality. It externalizes verified understanding produced earlier in the system.

What it is

  • • A customer-facing articulation of verified understanding
  • • An execution-aligned narrative
  • • A downstream artifact only

What it is not

  • • A discovery document
  • • A strategy substitute
  • • A standalone product

Where the RSO sits

The RSO exists only after SignalGate and Module One. It inherits their constraints and cannot override them.

SignalGate → Module One → RSO → Execution
