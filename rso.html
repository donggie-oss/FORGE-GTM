EXECUTION ARTIFACT

Recommended Solution Overview (RSO)

An RSO is not discovery, strategy, or diagnosis. It is the downstream externalization of verified understanding.

RSOs are produced only after SignalGate and Module One have established admissibility and operational reality.

What an RSO Is

  • • A structured execution artifact grounded in verified reality
  • • Evidence-led, account-specific, and operator-grade
  • • Designed to survive contact with the organization
  • • Built for decision-makers, not sales persuasion

What an RSO Is Not

  • • A discovery document or exploratory analysis
  • • A strategy deck produced upfront
  • • A standalone product you can purchase first
  • • A substitute for Module One

Where the RSO Fits

The RSO exists downstream of two gates: SignalGate determines whether an account is rational to pursue; Module One establishes operational reality and authority. Only then is an RSO produced.

The RSO amplifies whatever understanding exists. The gates ensure that understanding is real before anything is amplified.
