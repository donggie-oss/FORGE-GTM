HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS

The Forge GTM Method

The Forge GTM Method is a gated operating system for research, decision-making, and outbound execution — designed to prevent false confidence and wasted effort.

Each stage exists to answer a different question, in the correct order.

Gate 1
SignalGate
Determines whether an account is rational to pursue.
Gate 2
Module One
Establishes operational reality, constraints, and authority.
Output
RSO
A downstream execution artifact — never produced upfront.

Module One: Operational Reality

Module One exists to replace assumptions with evidence. It maps how the business actually operates — not how it is described in sales conversations.

  • • Leadership psychology and authority mapping
  • • Structural constraints and operational friction
  • • Revenue leakage and process breakdowns
  • • Explicit documentation of knowns vs unknowns

Why the Order Matters

Skipping gates creates false confidence. Producing execution artifacts before operational reality is understood leads to plans that cannot survive contact with the organization.

The method enforces discipline by design. You cannot advance until the previous question has been answered cleanly.
