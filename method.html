The Forge Method

A GTM method built to hold under pressure — not just in workshops.

The Forge Method is a three-module GTM system designed for founder-led and mid-market teams who need clean signal, disciplined outbound, and leadership guardrails that survive real constraints.

Module 1

Unified GTM & Fringe Diagnostic

We map how revenue is really created and lost across your lanes — including the “fringe” where deals, people, and signals quietly decay.

  • • Leadership psychology & decision map
  • • Tech stack, leakage points, and shadow tools
  • • Margin pools, risk zones, and non-negotiables
  • • Hidden “no-fail” dependencies in your funnel

Module 2

RSO Engine: Recommended Solution Overviews

We turn your value proposition into account-specific RSOs your team can actually use in outbound, exec meetings, and board conversations.

  • • Message architecture across your key segments
  • • Heavy RSOs that speak to real operators, not personas
  • • Evidence, risk, and scenario framing baked in
  • • No-ERP / no-buzzword language rules for the field

Module 3

Outbound Engine BOT: Build • Operate • Transfer

We build the outbound motion, run it with you under pressure, then transfer a proven engine your team can own without constant “hero” months.

  • • Outreach-ready account lists & named humans
  • • Email, DM, and penetration doctrine per lane
  • • Weekly operating rhythm & scoreboards
  • • Transfer plan so the engine survives after we exit
