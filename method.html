The Forge Method

A GTM architecture built to hold under pressure.

The Forge Method describes the operating systems behind SignalGate, RSOs, outbound execution, and leadership decision-making. These systems are not sold as a bundle.

These systems exist independently. Engagements begin and end based on signal — not progression.

Module 1

Unified GTM & Fringe Diagnostic

We map how revenue is actually created and lost across lanes — including the fringe where deals, people, and signals quietly decay.

  • • Leadership psychology & decision map
  • • Tech stack, leakage points, and shadow tools
  • • Margin pools, risk zones, and non-negotiables
  • • Hidden “no-fail” dependencies surfaced for review

SignalGate is a separate, per-account validation used to decide whether deeper diagnostic work is warranted.

Module 2

RSO Engine

Account-specific Recommended Solution Overviews are produced selectively for accounts that justify deeper investment.

  • • Evidence-backed, operator-grade narratives
  • • Consistent structure for emails, meetings, and boards
  • • Risk, scenario, and trade-off framing
  • • No-ERP / no-buzzword language rules

Module 3

Outbound Engine: Build • Operate • Transfer

We build the outbound motion, operate it under pressure, then transfer a proven engine your team can own without constant heroics.

  • • Outreach-ready account lists & named humans
  • • Email, DM, and penetration doctrine per lane
  • • Weekly operating rhythm & scoreboards
  • • Transfer plan so the engine survives after exit
