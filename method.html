The Forge Method

A GTM system built to hold under pressure — not just in workshops.

Forge GTM is not a menu of services. It is a sequenced decision system designed to prevent false confidence before execution begins.

Every downstream action is constrained by what is learned upstream. The method enforces order — and rejects anything that violates it.

SignalGate

SignalGate exists to answer one question before momentum sets in:

Is this account rational to pursue at all?

It evaluates publicly observable signals and returns a clear PURSUE or PASS. No strategy. No narrative. No next steps.

Module One (Required Gate)

Module One establishes operational reality before commitment is allowed.

  • • Leadership psychology and decision authority
  • • Operational constraints and leakage points
  • • Margin pools, risk zones, and non-negotiables
  • • Explicit unknowns that cannot be ignored

Module One cannot be skipped.

Recommended Solution Overviews (RSOs)

RSOs are downstream execution artifacts produced only after Module One.

They externalize verified understanding to earn attention and advance execution.

Any RSO produced without Module One would be invalid by definition.
