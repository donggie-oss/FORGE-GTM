The Forge Method

A GTM method built to hold under pressure — not just in workshops.

The Forge Method is a three-module GTM system designed for founder-led and mid-market teams who need clean signal, disciplined outbound, and leadership guardrails that survive real constraints.

Module 1

Unified GTM & Fringe Diagnostic

We map how revenue is really created and lost — including the “fringe” where deals, people, and signals quietly decay.

  • • Leadership psychology & decision map
  • • Tech stack, leakage points, and shadow tools
  • • Margin pools, risk zones, and non-negotiables
  • • Hidden dependencies in your funnel

Module 2

RSO Engine

We turn your value proposition into account-specific RSOs your team can actually use in outbound and leadership conversations.

  • • Message architecture by key segments
  • • Heavy RSOs for operators & finance
  • • Evidence & scenario framing baked in
  • • No-ERP / no-buzzword rules

Module 3

Outbound Engine BOT

We build your outbound motion, run it with you under pressure, then transfer a proven engine your team can own without hero selling.

  • • Outreach-ready lists & named humans
  • • Email, DM & penetration paths
  • • Weekly rhythm & scoreboards
  • • Full transfer plan
