PRICING

Pricing enforces order.

Forge GTM is a sequenced decision system. You don’t buy pieces out of order. Pricing exists to prevent execution on false assumptions.

Engagements proceed only after upstream gates are satisfied.

  • • Records evidence and explicit unknowns
  • • Returns a clear PURSUE or PASS

No strategy. No recommendations. No credit toward other work. If PASS, the engagement ends cleanly.

Recommended Solution Overview (RSO)
Execution artifact
$2,500

Assurance (credit-only)

SignalGate is conservative by design. If SignalGate returns PASS and you proceed anyway, and the deal closes successfully within 12 months (same core scope and buying center; no material facts withheld), you receive credits for 1 SignalGate, 1 Module One, and 1 RSO on any single account of your choosing.

Credits are not cash refunds and apply only under the conditions stated above.

Why you can’t buy an RSO yet

Most GTM failures don’t come from poor execution. They come from confident execution on false assumptions. An RSO is not a discovery product — it is the externalized output of verified understanding.

The sequence is the product: SignalGate → Module One → RSO. Pricing exists to enforce that order.
