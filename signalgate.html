SignalGate exists to answer that question before time, money, and credibility are committed.

What It Is Not

  • • Not consulting
  • • Not RevOps
  • • Not a recommendation engine

Ecosystem Scope

SignalGate is built on modular signal libraries. Validation confidence is currently strongest in Odoo-aligned environments. Additional ecosystems are incorporated only once their signal libraries meet the same validation standard.
