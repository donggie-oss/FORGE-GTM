SignalGate

A hard gate on account pursuit — before momentum sets in.

SignalGate exists to answer one question before time, money, and credibility are committed:

Is this account rational to pursue at all?

What SignalGate Does

  • • Evaluates publicly observable signals tied to operational reality
  • • Records evidence with explicit unknowns
  • • Issues a clear pursue/pass disposition

What SignalGate Does Not Do

  • • Does not recommend next steps
  • • Does not produce strategy or narrative
  • • Does not trigger deeper work
  • • Does not imply further engagement

If SignalGate returns PURSUE, the next question is no longer “should we chase this?” It becomes “do we understand reality well enough to commit?”

That is a different decision — and a different product.
