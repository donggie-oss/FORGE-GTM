Account Focus Validation

SignalGate

A disciplined filter that answers one question before momentum sets in:
Is this account rational to pursue at all?

What SignalGate Is

SignalGate validates account focus using publicly observable signals. It determines whether pursuit is rational, independent of execution quality, team skill, or sales narrative.

No internal access required. No assumptions. No storytelling.

What SignalGate Produces

  • • Account disposition: PURSUE or PASS
  • • Signal ledger tied to public evidence
  • • Operational signal summary
  • • Explicit, recorded unknowns

No recommendations. No next steps.

Each engagement includes a concise internal RSO-format brief documenting signal evidence, disposition, and unknowns so decisions remain reviewable and defensible.

What SignalGate Is Not

  • • Not a sales audit
  • • Not consulting
  • • Not RevOps
  • • Not a recommendation engine

Ecosystem Scope

SignalGate is built on modular signal libraries. Validation confidence is currently strongest in Odoo-aligned environments. Additional ecosystems are incorporated only once their signal libraries meet the same standard.
