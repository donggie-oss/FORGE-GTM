SignalGate

SignalGate determines whether an account is rational to pursue before time, money, and credibility are committed.

Public signals only. No narrative. No execution bias.

What SignalGate Is

  • • Binary account admissibility: PURSUE or PASS
  • • Publicly observable evidence only
  • • Explicit documentation of knowns and unknowns
  • • Independent of access or execution quality

What SignalGate Is Not

  • • No strategy
  • • No recommendations
  • • No execution guidance
  • • No sales narrative
  • • No follow-ups or "maybes"

SignalGate Output

PURSUE
PASS

No intermediate states. Discovery does not equal decision. Ambiguity defaults to PASS.

If we say PASS and you proceed anyway

SignalGate is conservative by design. If SignalGate returns PASS, you can still choose to pursue the account.

If you proceed anyway and the deal closes successfully within 12 months (with the same core scope and buying center, and with no material facts withheld), you receive credits for:

  • • 1 SignalGate run
  • • 1 full Module One

Credits are not cash refunds and are applied to any single account of your choosing, subject to the conditions above.
