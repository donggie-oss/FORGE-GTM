Account Focus Validation

SignalGate

Is this account worth pursuing at all?

SignalGate exists to answer that question before time, money, and credibility are committed.

What SignalGate Is

SignalGate validates account focus using publicly observable signals. It determines whether pursuit is rational, independent of execution quality.

It does not rely on internal access, assumptions, or sales narratives.

What SignalGate Produces

  • Account Disposition — PURSUE or PASS
  • Signal Ledger — Findings tied to public sources
  • Operational Signal Summary
  • Explicit Unknowns

No recommendations. No next steps.

Each SignalGate engagement includes a concise RSO-format brief — documenting signal evidence, disposition, and explicit unknowns — so decisions are recorded, reviewable, and defensible.

What SignalGate Is Not

  • Not a sales audit
  • Not consulting
  • Not RevOps
  • Not a recommendation engine

Ecosystem Scope

SignalGate is built on modular signal libraries. Validation confidence is currently strongest in Odoo-aligned environments. Additional ecosystems are incorporated only once their signal libraries meet the same validation standard.

Pressure-test account focus before momentum sets in

SignalGate engagements are conducted as working sessions. No demos. No pitches. Just a clear determination of whether pursuit is rational — or a mistake.
