Gate 1 — Account Admissibility

SignalGate

SignalGate enforces account admissibility before execution begins. It answers one question only: is this account rational to pursue?

Public signals only. Binary outcome. Fail-closed by default.

What SignalGate Is

  • • Binary admissibility decision: PURSUE or PASS
  • • Publicly observable evidence only
  • • Explicit recording of knowns, unknowns, and contradictions
  • • Independent of access, persuasion, or execution quality
