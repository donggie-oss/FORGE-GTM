SignalGate

SignalGate exists to answer a single question: Is this account rational to pursue?

Before strategy. Before execution. Before credibility is spent.

What SignalGate Is

SignalGate is the admissibility gate for the Forge GTM system. Its role is not to improve outcomes — but to prevent irrational bets.

  • • Binary account admissibility: PURSUE or PASS
  • • Based solely on publicly observable signals
  • • Independent of access, execution quality, or persuasion skill
  • • Produces a defensible record of knowns and explicit unknowns

What SignalGate Is Not

SignalGate deliberately excludes anything that would contaminate judgment.

  • • No strategy
  • • No recommendations
  • • No execution advice
  • • No sales narrative or positioning language
  • • No follow-ups, contingencies, or “maybes”

SignalGate Output

SignalGate produces a single, final determination.

PURSUE
PASS

There are no intermediate states. Discovery does not equal decision. Ambiguity defaults to PASS.
